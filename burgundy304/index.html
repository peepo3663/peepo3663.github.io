Welcome to Burgundy Residence

The property is named “Burgundy Residence”, as it is a part of the Burgundy Group, Thailand. This is the second branch of Burgundy Serviced Apartments. The first branch is located in Thonglor, Bangkok. Due to the huge success in attracting expatriates especially Japanese customers, the Burgundy Group has branch out to 304 Industrial Park. Therefore, customers can be fully confident in the high quality and personalized service offered.

Location less than 5 km away from 304 Industrial Park and a short distance from Big C, Tesco Lotus, 304 Market. Burgundy Residence is a luxurious and professionally maintained serviced apartment that offers top quality services and comfort living.
